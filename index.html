About Rubik's Cubes

The Rubik's Cube is a 3D combination puzzle invented in 1974 by ErnÅ‘ Rubik. It has become a worldwide sensation and a popular brain-teaser.

History of the Cube

The Rubik's Cube was initially called the "Magic Cube" and was later renamed after its inventor. Since its debut, over 350 million cubes have been sold worldwide.

Tips for Solving

  • Learn the basic notations: R (Right), L (Left), U (Up), D (Down), F (Front), B (Back).
  • Start with solving the white cross first.
  • Practice solving layer by layer.
  • Understand and memorize common algorithms.
